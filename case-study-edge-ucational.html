Vision & Opportunity

How might we make Edge's most advanced features feel effortless and inviting to everyday users? We created the Edge-ucational series—a campaign of friendly, bite-sized videos that helped users not only understand Edge, but love it.

"We turned product features into real-life storytelling moments that educated, delighted, and empowered."

Built in collaboration with the award-winning agency Buck, this project transformed complex browser capabilities into accessible, engaging content that resonates with users across all skill levels.
