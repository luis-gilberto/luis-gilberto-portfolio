Transforming Browsing with AI

Comprehensive campaign introducing AI-powered features in Microsoft Edge and Copilot integration

AI Integration Product Innovation User Experience

The Vision

As Microsoft ramped up early Copilot awareness in late 2023, the Edge team saw an opportunity to anchor its AI story in everyday browsing. This campaign launched just ahead of the holiday season, positioning Edge as the AI-powered browser that transforms how people interact with the web.

Strategic Positioning

Part of a broader Go-To-Market strategy, we positioned Edge not merely as a tool, but as a trusted AI companion in your digital life. From in-app demos to social-first video, this creative library became Edge's springboard for bringing smarter browsing to life.

Collaboration with Not Real

This campaign was brought to life in collaboration with the brilliant team at Not Real. Their animation and motion design expertise amplified our storytelling, creating visually compelling content that made complex AI concepts accessible and engaging.

The partnership focused on translating technical AI capabilities into human-centered narratives that resonated with everyday users, ensuring the campaign felt both innovative and approachable.

Reflection

This campaign marked a pivotal moment in Microsoft Edge's evolution, successfully positioning the browser as an AI-first platform rather than just another web browser. The strategic timing and comprehensive approach created a foundation for Edge's continued AI messaging.

By focusing on real-world value and human-centered storytelling, we transformed complex AI features into compelling reasons for users to choose Edge as their primary browser. The campaign's success demonstrated the power of strategic narrative architecture in technology marketing.
