Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Campaign development typically takes 4-8 weeks, while brand positioning projects can range from 6-12 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with startups?

Absolutely! I love working with startups and emerging brands. Whether you're launching your first campaign or scaling your marketing efforts, I can adapt my approach to fit your stage and budget.

What industries do you specialize in?

While I have deep experience in technology (Microsoft, AI, productivity tools), I've worked across various industries including healthcare, finance, and consumer goods. My strategic approach adapts to any sector.

How do you approach new projects?

Every project starts with discovery—understanding your goals, audience, and challenges. From there, I develop a strategic framework that guides creative execution, ensuring every touchpoint serves your objectives.
