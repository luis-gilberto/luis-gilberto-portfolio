Peace of Mind in Your Palm

In a year when "together" felt more precious than ever, we set out to show that true peace of mind can fit in the palm of your hand. The Family Safety launch campaign wove together heartfelt storytelling and playful motion graphics to demonstrate how Microsoft's new app keeps loved ones connected, protected, and empowered—no matter where life takes them.

The Challenge

We needed to position Microsoft Family Safety as the go-to tool for modern parents—showing that peace of mind can be simple, intuitive, and deeply human.

Reflection

As the world came to a screeching halt, we wanted to build something that did more than just roll out a new app, we wanted to fold technology into the family routine. When we launched Family Safety, families were coming to us to share that they used our app to finally get caught up on bedtimes (no more nagging about screen time), that they traded so many "Where are you?" texts for a collective exhale, and that they finally felt like they could all rest easy knowing everyone could be reached with a tap of a button. When we crunched numbers and listened to early feedback, our guiding light did not waiver: design and build with the goal of bringing families together, in the best and most chaotic times, by helping every family feel safe, seen and supported, no matter what unexpected twists life brought our way.
