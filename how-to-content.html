The Vision

As Creative Strategy lead for Microsoft Edge's educational content initiative, I developed a comprehensive framework that transformed complex browser features into engaging, accessible tutorials. This project bridged the gap between advanced functionality and everyday user needs, creating a scalable content system that drove significant feature adoption and user satisfaction.

Strategic Positioning

The strategy focused on making advanced browser capabilities feel intuitive and immediately valuable to users across all skill levels, prioritizing practical application over feature promotion.

Strategic Collaboration

This educational content initiative was developed through strategic collaboration between product teams, UX researchers, content specialists, and educational designers. The partnership ensured that every piece of content served both user needs and business objectives effectively.

By combining deep product knowledge with educational best practices, we created a content framework that has since been adopted across multiple Microsoft products, demonstrating the scalable value of user-centered educational design.

Strategic Reflection

This project reinforced the critical importance of treating educational content as a strategic asset rather than just support material. By prioritizing user value and practical application over feature promotion, we created content that genuinely serves users while driving business outcomes.

The success of this framework has influenced how we approach educational content across the organization, establishing new standards for user-centered learning experiences in technology products.
